lang="en" My favorite National team in the world

The best and my favorite national team is the Japanese national team.

The Japanese team was the first team I ever watched and I've been watching the since

The first match specifically was the Japan vs Brazil VNL 2023 game

They have the best roster

Masahiro Sekita the setter. Hes 5'9 and typically short for a volleyball player. Masahiro almost never makes any mistakes and is very dependable

Hes shorter than even the average libero.

Yuji Nishida is the opposite for Japan. He's left handed and is also shorter than the average opposite

He has a amazingly quick swing and is a server you hate trying to dig.

Next is one of the best liberos in the world and got the MVP award for the 2024 VNL, Tomohiro Yamamoto

He is a huge influnce for me and a great inspiration for me.

Ran Takahashi is a opposite hitter for Japan and he is still really young and has a lot to learn

He also typically shorter than the average player but still claimed his spot on the team.

Taishi Onodera is one of the many middle blockers in japan and stands 6'8.

He is a formidable wall and also an olympian

Finally is Yuki Ishikawa the opposite hitter for Japan. Ishikawa is a veteran in the sport and a scary opponent

Yuki is a very crafty and creative hitter like Ran Takahashi but has more experience than him
